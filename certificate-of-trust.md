# Certificate Of Trust

{% hint style="success" %}
Every BWS Solution provides a blockchain-verified Certificate of Trust. Use this certificate to demonstrate your customer's authenticity, trust, and transparency.
{% endhint %}

When an operation is completed, a [Certificate of Trust](certificate-of-trust.md) is generated as part of the results. This certificate shows your transaction's relevant information in a format you can easily share with your customers.
